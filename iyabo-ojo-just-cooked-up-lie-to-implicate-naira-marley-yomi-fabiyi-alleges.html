'Iyabo Ojo Just Cooked Up Lie To Implicate Naira Marley'

Nollywood actor, Yomi Fabiyi, has called out his colleague, Iyabo Ojo, over claims that embattled Nigerian singer, Azeez Fashola, popularly known as Naira Marley, drugged her children.

Naija News reported that the movie star, a few days ago, revealed that she has a personal vendetta against the controversial singer, beyond the case of the late singer, Mohbad.

Iyabo said she had thought the singer was a sensible person and would send her kids to his house.

According to her, Naira Marley allegedly laced her children’s drinks and food with drugs, and about three people testified to the incident.

The thespian added that after Mohbad gets justice and his case is completed, she would start her case with Naira Marley.

In a post via Instagram, Yomi Fabiyi questioned why Iyabo Ojo failed to fight for her children at the time the ‘Yahoo Boy’ crooner made such a move toward her children.

According to Yomi, no mother will keep silent in such a situation, insisting that the actress might have just instigated the lie to implicate Naira Marley.

He added that the National Drug Law Enforcement Agency and the Police should invite Iyabo Ojo’s children for questioning.

He wrote, “I don’t know why Iyabo was sending her children, who are adults, to Naira Marley’s house in the first place. She stated that Naira was putting drugs in their food, and She called herself a mother. She never came out to fight for her children then…maybe she just cooked up this lie to implicate Naira.

“Why was her daughter laced with drugs if she was not into drugs already? Which mother will keep quiet in such a situation? The NDLEA and the police heard what she said and they are keeping mute. How will this country get better? I expect The NDLEA to invite her to bring her children and investigate her”.
