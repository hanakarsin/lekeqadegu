Cardi B Defends Lizzo from Social Media Trolls Who Brought Her to Tears

  • Cardi B has defended rapper Lizzo after social media trolls made her cry after body-shaming her a few days ago
  • Lizzo cried on Instagram Live recently when she spoke about how negative comments on her timeline affected her
  • The Bodak Yellow hitmaker reacted to a clip of Lizzo tearfully talking about how the body-shamers made her life difficult

The US superstar Cardi B has taken to social media to defend Lizzo from social media trolls after she was brought to tears over online negativity.
