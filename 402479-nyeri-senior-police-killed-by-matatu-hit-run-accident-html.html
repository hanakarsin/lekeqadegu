Nyeri: Senior police killed by matatu in hit and run accident

- Inspector Sammy Kamitu Musembi met his death at around 9pm on the fateful evening along Marua-Nanyuki road

- The senior police officer was heading towards Marua according to a police report filed at Kiganjo station

- The identity of the driver and the vehicle which knocked down Musembi was not immediately established

Police in Nyeri central sub-county are looking for an unknown vehicle that killed a senior officer in a clear case of hit and run.

A police report which was filed at Kiganjo police station on Monday, February 8, showed that inspector Sammy Kamitu Musembi met his death at around 9pm on the fateful evening along Marua-Nanyuki road.

Musembi who was stationed at the National Police Campus, Kiganjo, was heading towards Marua when the said vehicle hit him from behind killing him on the spot.

With the said vehicle failing to stop after the accident, Musembi's body was discovered on the road and taken to Nyeri Provincial Hospital Mortuary awaiting autopsy.

A similar case involving a police officer was reported recently in which the victim was confirmed dead.

The suspect, Jackline Nduku Nzioki, who was driving a car registered KCU 291T, allegedly knocked down her victim, a police officer, Nickson Kiprono, at around 2am on December 26.
