Kate del Castillo - Age, Family, Bio

Mexican film and television actress who starred in the popular Telemundo series La Reina del Sur. In 1998, she was nominated for a TVyNovelas Award for Best Young Lead Actress for her role in La Mentira. In 2017, she began starring as the First Lady of Mexico in the Netflix drama Ingobernable.

Before Fame

She debuted in the 1980 film The Last Escape.

Trivia

She was named one of the 50 most beautiful people in 2007 by People en Espanol magazine. In 2015, alongside Sean Penn, she had an encounter with Mexican drug lord El Chapo in his hideout.

Family Life

She was married to football player Luis Garcia from 2001 to 2004 and to actor Aaron Diaz from 2009 to 2011.

Associated With

She starred alongside Jennifer Lopez in the 2006 film Bordertown.
