What Is RHONJ Star Jennifer Aydin's Net Worth?

'RHONJ's' Jennifer Aydin Is No Joke, and Neither Is Her Net Worth

She fell into the role of being a Real Housewife easily, but that's not the only thing she has going on for herself.

By Sheridan Singleton

Published June 25 2024, 10:08 a.m. ET

News broke in September 2023 that both Danielle Cabral and Jennifer Aydin got into a physical altercation while filming Season 14 of The Real Housewives of New Jersey. It was also reported that both Danielle and Jennifer were suspended from filming. The suspension didn't last long, however. Now that the explosive fight has aired, viewers are taking sides.

Jennifer first joined the RHONJ cast in its ninth season. She quickly joined the ranks of the messiest cast members with the likes of Teresa Giudice. This is probably why they also get along so well. Jennifer has been a staunch supporter of Teresa since joining the series. She fell into the role of being a Real Housewife easily, but that's not the only thing she has going on for herself. In addition to having a very successful husband, she is also a businesswoman.

What is Jennifer Aydin's net worth?

According to Parade, Jennifer Aydin's net worth sits at a very healthy $11 million. This makes her the housewife with the highest net worth in her franchise. Jennifer has her own business, in addition to being a real housewife. She launched and began selling a pillow called the Ultimate Beauty Pillow, which was specifically designed for people who frequently get Botox and other facial fillers. Prior to joining the RHONJ, she also owned her jewelry store but now has new projects to focus on.
