Diamond's father defends Queen Darleen's decision to get married as second wife

- Mzee Abdul stated Islam religion allows a man to marry up to four wives

- He said people condemning her are the same people who would be trolling her if she was not married

- The dad added he is happy his daughter finally got married as it was unexpected

Diamond Platinumz's father Mzee Abdul has slammed people condemning his daughter Queen Darleen for being married as a second wife.

The old man said people should be happy for his daughter for the blessing instead of trolling her and making their own conclusions.

Speaking to Wasafi TV barely a week after Queen Darleen's wedding with billionaire businessman Isihaka, Mzee Abdul held his daughter is on the right track and has nothing to be ashamed of since Islam religion allows a man to marry up to four wives.

He added he is the happiest man in the world having seen her daughter wed, when he least expected.

''You know marriage is a very difficult thing. I am happy for her and people should be too. I never thought she would be married because of her music but here she is,'' Mzee Abdul stated.''She is just fine, I am wondering why people are making noise about her being married as a second wife. In Islam, there is nothing like one wife, a man can have as many as four,'' he added.
