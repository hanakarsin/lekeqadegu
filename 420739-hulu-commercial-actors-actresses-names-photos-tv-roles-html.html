1. Aaron Donald

Aaron Charles Donald is a renowned American football defensive tackle in the National Football League. He was born on May 23rd, 1991, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, United States. He plays for the Los Angeles Rams.

He plays tennis in a new Hulu commercial dubbed Time To Have Hulu. You can probably imagine that he would make a pretty good tennis player. Unfortunately, it's too bad the ad only shows Donald returning a few volleys.

Donald is a three-time Defensive Player of the Year, six-time All-Pro, and seven-time Pro Bowler. He has accomplished almost everything except the elusive Super Bowl.

Kris Jenner in a Hulu commercial is the mother of the Kardashians. In the TV commercial - Time To Have Hulu, Kris, Andra Day, and Aaron Donald have their Hulu preconceptions broken. They discover that everything that they love is so accessible and are delighted.
