King T-rell - Age, Family, Bio

Rapper, businessman and reality television personality who has released singles called "Vice City Remix" and "Get Out Of My Face," to name a few. He is a business partner of Tyga in Last Kings and was on Tyga's MTV series Kingin' With Tyga.

Before Fame

He dropped an earlier track called "True Dat" in 2014.

Trivia

He has a strong presence on Instagram with more than 340,000 followers to go along with 10,000 Twitter followers.

Family Life

He has a daughter named Zoe Skye and a son named Zane Supreme with Heather Sanders. He also has a daughter named Tylee from a previous relationship.

Associated With

His girlfriend Heather was previously a good friend of Blac Chyna before they had a falling out.
