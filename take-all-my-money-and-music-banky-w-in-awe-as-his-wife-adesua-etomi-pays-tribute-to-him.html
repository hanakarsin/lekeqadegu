'Take All My Money And Music'

Nollywood actress cum singer, Adesua Etomi has surprised her husband, Banky W after recreating one of his hit songs.

Naija News reports that Adesua took to her Instagram page to share a video of the Acapella version of  Banky W’s 2009 hit song, ‘Strong Thing’.

According to the mother of one, making the video was stressful but it was fun for her.

Adesua dedicated the song to Banky W, who she described as an insanely talented husband.

She wrote: “Did this as a tribute to my insanely talented husband @bankywellington Everything you hear was recorded by me, by mouth. It was stressful but fun. I activated the “W” in me that was hidden. Hat, Suit, Screw Face, Moves”

Banky W who seemed excited over the song teased his wife and wrote: “@adesuaetomi take all my money and my music!!!”

MC Oluomo Gifts Actor Igwe Olaiya Car

Meanwhile, the All Progressives Congress (APC) Chieftain and transport executive, MC Oluomo has gifted veteran Nollywood actor Igwe Olaiya a Mercedes Benz.

MC Olumo’s son made this known in a video shared on his Instagram story.

The video shows the moment Igwe Olaiya was presented with the SUV he ran in excitement, prostrating and rolling on the floor.
