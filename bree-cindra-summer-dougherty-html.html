Bree Cindra Summer Dougherty - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok star and sixth oldest child of the Dougherty Dozen, a family of 12 that rose to fame on TikTok. Her mother, Alicia Lee Dougherty, posts family vlogs, adoption, and meal prep content on their doughertydozen TikTok. The account has garnered over 6 million followers. 

Before Fame

In 2019, she was adopted along with her brother, Patrick, from a residential treatment facility.

Trivia

In September 2021, her mother posted a TikTok about what Bree eats in a day. It earned over 1 million views. In her spare time, Bree enjoys horseback riding.

Family Life

Her mother, Alicia, and father, Joshua Brian Dougherty, have ten children. She has three older brothers, four younger brothers, and two younger sisters. She is the biological sister of Patrick John Dougherty.

Associated With

Her mother's viral meal prep TikToks are often set to the song "9 to 5" by Dolly Parton.
