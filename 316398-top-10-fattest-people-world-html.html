Top 10 fattest people in the world. Who is the fattest person?

Ever imagined who the fattest people in the world are and how much they weigh? There are quite a number of them out there whose weights are utterly shocking. It is no joke because being considered among the fattest in the world means they have some considerable mass covered by the skins and they are certainly bothered about it.

Being fats is associated with several health problems; if it is not blood pressure, we could think of type-2 diabetes or many other diseases. There are so many factors which may contribute to weight gain, and excessive eating is just one of them. Some people become fat, and it stops at a certain weight, others bear the burden of seeing their weight get out of control to double or even triple.

Fattest people in the world

While it might be a bother to gain a kilo or two in a while to some of us, the struggle others go through is in an entirely different league. For this faction of people, they are looking at about hundreds of kilos. To them, their battle is no longer about shape. It is about life. These are some of the world’s fattest people with us today.

1. Paul Merson

Paul Merson was once considered the fattest person in the world. At that time, Merson was weighing about 980 pounds (444.5 Kg). His weight problem began when he suffered a heart attack at the time of his father’s death and deterioration of his mother’s health. He, however, managed to undergo gastric surgery and the weight went down to around 560 pounds.

2. Patrick Deuel

Think of someone being referred to as a 1000 pound man! Patrick Deuel could be the one. He weighed 1072 pounds and was commonly known as ‘Half-ton man’ among his peers, family members, and the general public. Patrick Deuel had to undergo gastric surgery to save his life. After the operation, he lost a considerable amount of weight, but he is still obese with a lot of health-related issues.

3. Donna Simpson

Donna Simpson, at some point, wanted to be the heaviest woman in the world. Her target weight was 1000 pounds (450 Kg). To show her determination, she went ahead to set up a website where her fans would watch her eat. Her efforts were rewarded in 2007 when she was honored by the Guinness World Record holder for the ‘heaviest woman to give birth.’ She had given birth to her daughter through caesarian.

4. Khalid bin Mohsen Shaari

In his late teenage years, Khalid had ballooned to unimaginable 1345 pounds (610 Kg) and was making news headlines. He was at one point, the world’s heaviest man. 5. Juan Pedro Franco

Juan Pedro Franco of Mexico was so obese that he had been bed-bound for nearly a decade before he finally received gastric bypass. His overweight condition was partly because of a severe thyroid condition. He left his homestead weighing 595 Kg to undergo surgery at a specialized clinic in Guadalajara, Jalisco. After two successive operations, he managed to lose over 304 Kg. He finally lost about 140 kg after his last surgery and was able to get out of bed and walk.
