Angie Arizaga - Age, Family, Bio

Model and social media influencer who is known for being the television host of the series La Previa. Her modeling content extends to Instagram, where she has over 4.4 million followers.

Before Fame

She first began sharing content to her Instagram in September of 2014. 

Trivia

 She has also modeled for companies such as Veca Nails, Balance Peru, and Sabz Luxury Swimwear. 

Family Life

She was born and raised in Lima, Peru. She began a relationship with Jota Benz. She previously dated Fabio Agostini and fellow TV show host Nicola Porcella. She has a pet dog named Thor. 

Associated With

One of her Instagram videos features clips of her on a float at Pride Fest in 2022, which she set to the song "Break my Soul" by Beyoncé.
