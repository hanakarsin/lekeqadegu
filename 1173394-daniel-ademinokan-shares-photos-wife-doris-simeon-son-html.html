Daniel Ademinokan shares photos with ex-wife Doris Simeon and son

Nollywood movie director Daniel Ademinokan has reunited with his ex-wife Doris Simeon and their son. The actress and Ademinokan were spotted having fun together with their son.

Ademinokan took to Instagram to share photos of them at a restaurant. On the post, the movie director stated that he had to spend time with his ex-wife and son after working in the studio all weekend.

The director who split with the actress in 2010, took the opportunity to advice people not to believe everything they read online.

He said: "Spent the entire weekend buried in a studio editing but I had to come up for air for THIS. Don't believe everything you read on blogs. They've gotta stay sensational to make a dollar. . It’s all good in the Hood."
