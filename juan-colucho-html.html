Juan Colucho - Age, Family, Bio

Leading man for the crime drama La Piloto whose first major role on television was for Televisa's A que no me dejas.

Before Fame

He appeared in two episodes of La Rosa de Guadalupe between 2013 and 2014. He had uncredited roles in Mi Corazón Es Tuyo and De que te quiero, te quiero.

Trivia

He stage credits include Cuatro XXXX (2013) and Tengamos el sexo en paz (2014).

Family Life

His full name is Juan Eduardo Colucho. He was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

Associated With

He stars in La Piloto alongside Livia Brito.
