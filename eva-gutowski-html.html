Eva Gutowski - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube sensation known for her channel Mylifeaseva where she posts videos about all things relating to beauty. She has accumulated more than 11 million subscribers. In 2019, she began hosting the web series How to Survive: A Break-Up. 

Before Fame

Growing up she dreamed of being an actress or possibly a psychiatrist. But, as she got older, she knew she wanted to be an entertainment host and reporter.

Trivia

She worked as a cashier in a mom and pop pizza shop and studied journalism in college. She has a secondary YouTube channel called Vlogtowski. She also has a popular TikTok account under the username mylifeaseva where she primarily posts lip sync videos for over 7 million fans. 

Family Life

She was born in Orange County and has a sister named Maya. In 2020, she began dating Olav Stubberud. She was in a relationship with Brent Rivera in 2019 and she dated Adam Bartoshesky from 2016 until 2018. She also previously dated Andrew Kingsbury and Alex Hayes. 

Associated With

She starred on the Hulu show All Night with Brec Bassinger.
