Chauncey Koziol

pinkpantheress - Age, Family, Bio

Musician known for her incorporation of jungle music and soft vocals into pop tunes. She is popular on TikTok, and has over 500,000 followers and 7 million likes. She has been signed by Elektra Records and Parlophone. 

Before Fame

She had her first breakout hit with her song "Break It Off," which was released on June 4, 2021. Her single, "Passion," was released in July 2021. 

Trivia

She got her moniker while watching The Chase when someone asked "What is a female panther called?" Pink Panther is also one of her favorite movies. In June of 2021, she posted a picture of a My Chemical Romance themed cake. 

Family Life

Her mother is Kenyan. 

Associated With

She has named Lily Allen and Imogen Heap as inspirational artists. She also has a dream of collaborating with pop artist Charli XCX.
