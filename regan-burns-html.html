Regan Burns - Age, Family, Bio

Comedian and actor best known for his appearances in numerous commercials, including ones for Arby's, Capital One, and Enterprise Rent-A-Car. He also portrayed arsonist Alan Shepard on Halfway Home and appeared on Reno 911!

Before Fame

He made his acting debut on TV's Suddenly Susan in 1999. He made his film debut the following year, playing Lance in The Best Man.

Trivia

He starred alongside G Hannelius and Blake Michael in the Disney series Dog with a Blog.

Family Life

He married Jennifer Burns in 1995. The couple has two children.

Associated With

He guest-starred on a 2011 episode of Weeds, appearing opposite Mary-Louise Parker.
