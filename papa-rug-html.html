Papa Rug - Age, Family, Bio

Father of Faze Clan director Brian Awadis, AKA FaZe Rug, and YouTube phenomenon Brandon Awadis. He and his wife also run their own YouTube channel titled Mama Rug and Papa Rug. The channel has featured a range of fun family videos and it has earned over 1.5 million subscribers. 

Before Fame

Originally from Iraq, he would raise his family in San Diego, California. 

Trivia

He has over 300,000 followers on his papaawadis Instagram account, which predominantly features pictures of himself alongside members of his family. He has also posted multiple photos posing in front of cars and multiple photos at sports games. 

Family Life

His name is Ron Awadis. His wife is known on the web as Mama Rug. In 2017, he reunited with a sister who he hadn't seen in 25 years.

Associated With

His son Brian and FaZe Dirty both became directors for the FaZe Clan.
