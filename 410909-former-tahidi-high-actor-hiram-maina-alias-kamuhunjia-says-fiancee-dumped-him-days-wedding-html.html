Former Tahidi High Actor Hiram Maina Alias Kamuhunjia Says Fiance Dumped Him Days to Wedding

- Actor and TV producer Hiram Maina alias Kamuhunjia spoke of being dumped by his fiancée

- Their families had even met, but shortly before the wedding, she called it quits

- Hiram said she did not explain why she left him

They say love is a beautiful thing, but it also exposes one to heartbreak. Former Tahidi High actor and TV producer Hiram Maina has spoken about how his fiancée dumped him shortly before their scheduled wedding.

Speaking on his YouTube channel, Hiram recounted how a promising love story turned into a nightmare.

According to the communications graduate, he popped the question, and things were looking up for them.

“I was in a serious relationship. I proposed to a woman I loved, and she said yes. We went for family introductions, and then life happened,” he said.

Hiram said that they had even planned their wedding when she said she could not go ahead with it out of the blues.

“My now ex-girlfriend just told me we couldn't continue with the relationship. When I looked at her finger, she did not have the engagement ring. Those words crushed me because I did not see it coming. We were planning to have our wedding in the next two months. It pained me,” said Hiram.

Dealing with heartbreak

Heartbroken, Hiram dealt with the pain the best way he knows how to, prayer.

“I am very prayerful, and when she ended the relationship, I said it is well, and I would get over it. I knew it was God's purpose,” he stated.

Healing

Hiram further divulged that it took him longer to heal since she did not explain why she called it quits.

“You cannot just tell someone it is over. You should have a tangible reason. If I knew the reason, maybe I could have healed a long time ago. She left me wondering what I had done wrong. I have never known the reason why she left. But our God is gracious,” said Hiram.

Aside from an acting stint on Tahidi High as Hiram, he plays Kamuhunjia (preacher) in a show on Inooro TV, hence the nickname.
