Why I Am In BBNaija 'All-Stars' Edition Despite Being Married

Reality TV star Chukwuemeka Okoye, popularly called Frodd, has revealed his decision to join the Big Brother Naija ‘All Stars’ edition despite his marriage.

Naija News reports that Frodd, during his appearance at the show premiere on Sunday, July 23rd, said he is in the show for his wife.

He said, “I’m here for my woman and we had to talk over some things before coming on the show.”

According to him, his wife has always loved the BBNaija show, and they hope to win the N120 million grand prize.

Frodd is expecting a baby with his wife.
