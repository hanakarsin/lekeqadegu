Dror Soref - Age, Family, Bio

Popular Israeli-born writer, producer, and director. He directed The Seventh Coin, a film starring Peter O'Toole, and produced Basic, a 2003 Samuel L. Jackson and John Travolta movie.

Before Fame

During his time as a sociology, economics, and anthropology student at the University of Haifa in Israel, he founded a theater company.

Trivia

After attending the University of Southern California's film school, he directed a music video for then-unknown performer "Weird Al" Yankovic.

Family Life

He was born in Israel to a family of Spanish heritage. He married Virginia Pereira in 2000.

Associated With

He produced, directed, and co-wrote Not Forgotten, a 2009 film that starred Paz Vega.
