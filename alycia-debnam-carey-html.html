Alycia Debnam-Carey - Age, Family, Bio

After rising to fame on the reality series Next Stop Hollywood, she had a recurring role on The CW series The 100 and played significant roles in the films Into the Storm and The Devil's Hand. 

Before Fame

Early in her screen acting career, she appeared in episodes of McLeod's Daughters and Dance Academy. 

Trivia

In 2015, she took on the starring role of Alicia Clark on AMC's Fear the Walking Dead. 

Family Life

A native of Sydney, Australia, she studied music at the Newtown High School of the Performing Arts. 

Associated With

She co-starred with Brit Morgan in a 2015 German film titled Unfriend.
