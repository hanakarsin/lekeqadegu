Bree Smith biography

Bree Smith was born on December 28, 1981, in Denver, Colorado. The name of her father is Stuart Smith. Her father split up with her mother, and her mother got married again. She shares a very strong bond with her stepfather, John Eblen.

Bree Smith education

For her high school education, she attended the San Ramon Valley High School. After graduating from high school, she enrolled at the Southern Methodist University in Dallas, Texas to pursue a degree in Theatre Performance. Bree graduated in 2005. She spent three years working as a professional actress both on stage and film in New York City.

Her love of weather prompted her to return to school to obtain a degree in meteorology. She enrolled at Florida State University to pursue a degree in meteorology and graduated with honours. In 2008, she transferred to Florida International University where she graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Meteorology in 2010.

Bree Smith career

Bree debuted her career when she was hired to work as an intern at WPLG-TV. At that time, she was studying in Florida. The internship lasted for four months, from May to August 2009.

After graduation, she was hired to work as an intern with WDSU-NBC. In 2010, she and her husband relocated to New Orleans. Shortly after arriving in New Orleans, she started working as the weekend meteorologist for WVLA-TV in Baton Rouge, Louisiana.

She was the primary fill-in for weekday morning and evening shows, responsible for live weather and traffic reports across multiple station platforms. Bree was responsible for weathercasts for 5 PM/10 PM weekend shows.

In 2011, she relocated to her husband's hometown of St. Louis where she was hired by KSDK-TV as the weekend meteorologist. She presented the weather for the channel for five years. Bree spent her time tracking tornadoes and winter storms. Smith also reported on the historic New Years flooding of 2016.
