Neil Robertson - Age, Family, Bio

World Snooker Champion who is widely considered the best Australian snooker player ever. He is one of the few snooker players ever to have won the "triple crown" consisting of the Masters, U.K. Championship and World Championship titles.

Before Fame

He started playing snooker at age fourteen and began his professional career in 1998.

Trivia

In 2013-14, he became the first snooker player ever to make one hundred centuries in a season.

Family Life

He has a son named Alexander.

Associated With

He defeated English player Ronnie O'Sullivan at the 2015 Masters.
