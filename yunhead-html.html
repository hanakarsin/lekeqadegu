Yun Head - Age, Family, Bio

Musician, rapper and producer who is best recognized for his satirical meme raps. Some of his most popular songs include "Trap Anthem" and "Cats On Mars."  

Before Fame

He began releasing music in 2018. Before gaining fame as Yun Head, he made parodies under the moniker Roblox da Gamer.

Trivia

His "Trap Anthem" lyric video has been viewed over 17 million times.

Family Life

He was raised in Hawaii. 

Associated With

In March 2019, he released a video on his secondary yunheadtv YouTube channel titled, "Roblox da Gamer - Roblox On My Mind [YNW Melly "Murder On My Mind" ROBLOX Parody]."
