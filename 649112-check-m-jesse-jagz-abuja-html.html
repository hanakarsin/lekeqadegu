Abuja Fashion Week Kicks Off With New Designers

The first night of Abuja Fashion Week featured upcoming designers.

The Abuja Fashion Week kicked off on Wednesday, November 25, showcasing budding fashion designers for its first night. The first three of whom were Tunurlz, Juwairah (whose collection featured mostly long flowing gowns) and Jummy Giwa, a student.

The fourth and only male designer of the night was BZ Design with his amazing kaftans, while Karen closed out the night with her Diamen label.
