Justin Kroma - Age, Family, Bio

One of two personalities behind the YouTube channel LankyBox. He and his partner Adam McArthur are also known as The Zero Budget Guys as they are known for videos such as "K-Pop With Zero Budget," "Ariana Grande With Zero Budget!" and other musical performers with zero budget series. 

Before Fame

He and Adam made their YouTube debut with a faux America's Got Talent video called "GREATEST TALENT AUDITION EVER!!!" 

Trivia

The duo's YouTube channel has accrued more than 18 million subscribers while his personal Instagram account gained 80,000 followers. 

Family Life

He resided in Seattle, Washington. 

Associated With

He and Adam made "Zero Budget" videos about BTS, BLACKPINK, Billie Eilish, Shawn Mendes and many others.
