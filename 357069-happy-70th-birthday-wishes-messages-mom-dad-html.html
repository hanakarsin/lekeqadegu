Happy 70th birthday wishes, messages for mom and dad

Parents are an irreplaceable source of joy, no matter their age. They naturally fall in the circle of our most loved ones for their immeasurable commitments and sacrifices. Living to see your parents grow old is a blessing that no one should take for granted; therefore, letting your parents know you deeply love them and value their precious years is essential. Dad and mom should get the best for a happy 70th birthday.

Appreciation messages and wishes are the most appropriate way of celebrating our parents during their birthdays. The powers of thoughtful, genuine wishes surpass even the most precious gift. Including a note with sincere wishes will leave your mom and dad with a youthful smile.

Happy birthday wishes for 70-year-old parents

Choosing a perfect message to share with your parent or guardian is easier once you establish your intent. Do not let distance stop you from showering your mom or dad with the best 70th birthday wishes. Remember that thoughtful words of appreciation can cross several miles to touch hearts in your absentia.

Happy 70th birthday dad

What do you say for a 70th birthday? Think of how lucky you are to have your dad and come up with thoughtful words to let him know how much you value him after all he has done to shape your life.

  • Our family would not have been the same without you! We celebrate you on this special day. Happy birthday, dad.
  • You have been the best dad, a fearless family builder, a responsible role model, and above all, a precious gift from God. Happy 70th birthday papa.
  • I have an assurance to count on you always. Thank you for being the best. Happy birthday, dad!
  • Happy birthday wishes to the greatest dad we know. We are always thankful you raised us with an abundance of love. If we had the alternative of choosing our parents, we will choose you again and again.
  • May God shower you with grace and blessings as you celebrate your admirable life. Happy 70th birthday, dad.
  • You have been the strongest pillar in our lives. May your 70th birthday bring good memories and tidings in your heart. Happy birthday our king!
  • We could never have wished for another dad because your fatherly wisdom and love are sufficient. Happy birthday, dad!
  • We are proud to celebrate you at 70 for being our protector and provider. We will always cherish your fatherly role in our hearts. Stay blessed as you turn a year older.
  • We will forever love you, dad! May God increase your joy and peace as you turn a year older, Happy 70th birthday dad.
  • Receive our deepest wishes on your 70th birthday! You will forever remain unforgettable in our hearts.
  • Dad, you mean the universe to me. Your love, generosity, and patience saw me through the toughest times in life, and I consider myself richly blessed to have you as my father. Happy birthday dad.

Happy 70th birthday mom

Moms are the best caregivers we can ever have in life because they only become better as they age. Even if you have your differences, shower your mama with more love on her birthday to let her know her place in your life.

  • Have the happiest 70th birthday, mom! The special bond we share means everything to my life. I would be lost long ago without a caring mother like you!
  • You are a special mother in our lives! Feel loved as you blow your 70th birthday candles with lots of joys and presents!
  • You mean so much to us for your tenderness and loving care. We are lucky to have a wonderful mother who has won the deepest of our caring thoughts—happy 70th birthday, mom.
  • We may have caused your stresses and pains growing up, but you never gave up being a good mother. We wish you more fruitful and beautiful years to continue enjoying the fruits of your labor—happy 70th birthday mom.
  • We wish you a happy birthday even as you turn 70 today! We could not have a better mother. Enjoy your special day with lots of gifts, blessings, and joy.
  • We are proud of you for being our friend and mom. May you have a long life to experience all of God’s blessings. We wish you lots of blessings, chocolate, and cakes on your 70th birthday.
  • You are my best friend for always being there for me. May God meet all the desires of your heart as you celebrate your special day. Happy birthday, mom.
  • Happy birthday, mum! May you find favor as you celebrate your 70th birthday. We wish you a wonderful birthday and life to blow many more candles.
  • I celebrate you, my hero, for turning 70 years! Just to appreciate your input in my life. I am always thankful that God gave us a loving mother like you. Happy birthday, mum.
