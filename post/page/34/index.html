22 Justices Nominated For Elevation To Supreme Court

Jun 7, 2024 The National Judicial Council (NJC) has received a list of 22 justices of the Court of Appeal nominated by the Federal Judicial Service Commission (FJSC) for elevation to the Supreme Court. Tinubu Picks Date To Present Shettima

Jun 7, 2024 The presidential candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Bola Tinubu has reportedly picked a date to present his running mate, Senator Kashim Shettima to the party’s leadership. Naija News gathered that except for any last-minute change that may happen, Tinubu will officially present Shettima to the APC National Working Committee (NWC) and its National Chairman, Senator Abdullahi Adamu on Wednesday, 20th July. The presentation is expected to take place at the APC National Secretariat in Abuja.
