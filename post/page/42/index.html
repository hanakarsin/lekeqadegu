Tinubu Speaks On Reforming Almajiri System

May 23, 2024 If elected as the Nigerian leader in 2023, the Presidential candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Bola Tinubu, has promised to reform the Almajiri system. Naija News reports Almajiri is a system of Islamic education practised in Northern part of Nigeria where male teenagers leave the comfort of their parents to seek Islamic knowledge. However, the Almajiri has been widely criticised by many leaders as the boys are visibly seen on the streets of all major cities in the North littering motor parks, ATM centres, hotels, brothels, gridlocks and even religious places to beg for alms.
