Stima Sacco loans - Types, interest rates, and requirements

For many Kenyans, Sacco loans are the best as they usually have lower interest rates, lower insurance premiums, and exciting partner discounts. These institutions also give one high returns on deposits, investment opportunities, and a chance to build a cash reserve much faster than most banks. True to the assertions, Stima Sacco loans have a much lower interest rate, friendly repayment periods, and higher limits.

Members can literally access a wide variety of financing options regardless of whether they have Stima Sacco shares, alpha deposits, or prime account types. However, you have to meet their minimum requirement criteria to have your requested amount approved in the shortest time possible.

Depending on the magnitude of your credit, you have the following options:

Walk-in application

For faster process we recommend you to follow the steps below:

  • Download and print the Stima Sacco application form
  • Fill it in: Provide your personal information, employment details, loan particulars, security data, and agree to the terms and conditions.
  • Walk into any branch and submit the duly filled copy with supporting original and photocopy documents such as national I.D. and payslip.
  • Wait for your credit request to be verified.

Stima Sacco app

This is most suited for short term personal emergency loans. You will need to:

  • Navigate to Google Play Store
  • Search for the ‘M-Pawa’ application.
  • Download and install it
  • Login to your account
  • Follow on-screen instructions to get your credit.

Interest=principal amount X rate/100 X time

  • Principal = Ksh. 1,000,000
  • Rate = 9.6
  • Time = 2 years (24 months)

Hence,

= 1,000,000 X (9.6)/100 X 2 years

Your interest will be Ksh. 192,000

The total amount payable will be: interest + principal

For our case = 1,000,000 + 192,000 = Ksh. 1,192,000

So, the Sacco will owe you Ksh. 1,192,000
