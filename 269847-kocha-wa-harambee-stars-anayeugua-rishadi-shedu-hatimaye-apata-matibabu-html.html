Kocha wa Harambee Stars anayeugua Rishadi Shedu hatimaye apata matibabu

-Shedu alipata nafuu wakati marafiki wake na wahisani walimsaidia ili kupata matibabu

-Walifanikiwa kupata KSh 1.8 milioni kugharamia matibabu hayo India
