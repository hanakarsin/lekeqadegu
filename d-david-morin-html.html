D. David Morin - Age, Family, Bio

Television and film actor known primarily for appearing in CSI: Miami, Dexter, The Mentalist, Matlock, Touched by an Angel and Chuck. He received an award for Best Film Under 30 Minutes at the Damah Film Festival in 2001. 

Before Fame

He began his professional acting career in 1984. 

Trivia

In high school, he took Tom Hanks' wife, Rita Wilson, to prom at Hollywood High School.

Family Life

He is the son of actress Marty Morin and the nephew of actress Patricia Ellis.

Associated With

He appeared alongside David Caruso in CSI: Miami.
