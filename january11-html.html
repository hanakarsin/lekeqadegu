January 11 Horoscope and Zodiac

The Capricorn's ruling planet is Saturn, but as you were born in the third Decan, or part, of the sign, Mercury also lends you some of its mysterious planetary influence. While Saturn is linked to your determination, discipline and organization, it is Mercury's power that links to your exceptional communication skills and agile mind. Your unique combination of planetary influences makes you more intellectually driven than the other Capricorn Decans. With a shrewd appreciation for detail, order and structure, there are many accomplishments that lay ahead of you. In love, you are extremely loyal and will allow loved ones to become fully integrated into your life. Unfortunately, your strong commitment makes it hard to forget when others have wronged you. Fight the urge to hold grudges, as this only detracts from your happiness.
