May 18, 2024 Chauncey Koziol

ItsFunneh - Age, Family, Bio

Content creator on YouTube named Kat who creates amusing roleplay, adventure, mini-game and challenge videos from the games Minecraft and Roblox. She has earned more than 9 million subscribers. She and her siblings formed the gaming group known as The Krew.

Before Fame

She joined the YouTube world in September of 2011. She did not publish her first video, "Thirsty Crafter (Minecraft Machinima Short)," until New Years Day of 2013. 

Trivia

She created a popular series for her YouTube channel titled Yandere High School. She has also posted a number of videos centered around Fairy High School. One of them, which is focused on the first day at Fairy High School, has become one of her most popular videos with over 35 million views. 

Family Life

Her sisters are gamers GoldenGlare, PaintingRainbows and LunarEclispe. Her brother is gamer DraconiteDragon.  

Associated With

In July 2018, she released a YouTube video titled, "Human Fall Flat - SHE'S TRYING TO KILL US! w/ Gloom (Funny Moments)."
