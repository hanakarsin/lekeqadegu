Cho Mi-yeon - Age, Family, Bio

Korean pop singer who has gained fame as one of the main vocalists for the K-Pop girl band (G)I-DLE. She was featured in rapper Kid Milli's song "Kitty" in November 2021. In April of 2022, she released her own EP titled My. 

Before Fame

She first auditioned to perform in middle school.

Trivia

She can play the violin and piano. She appeared on the variety travel show, Seoul Connects U, in November 2020. 

Family Life

She was first encouraged to love music by her father.

Associated With

She has been signed to the same label as Jo Kwon.
