The first season of The Real Housewives of New York City reboot proved to be a success, making the anticipation for Season 15 all the greater. The first season was an important test to see if Bravo would be able to successfully reboot a tried and true Real Housewives franchise. Rebecca Minkoff also joins as a new friend of the Housewives, and she's got a pretty impressive net worth as well. So, what is Rebecca Minkoff's net worth?
