APC Lawmaker Visits Bola Tinubu In London, UK [Photo]

A member of the House of Representatives, Usman Zanna, has paid a visit to the National Leader of the All Progressives Congress, APC, Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

Naija News reports that Zanna, a first-time lawmaker who represents Gubio, Kaga, Magumeri federal constituency of Borno State, paid a visit to Tinubu in London on Tuesday.

The former Lagos State Governor is rumoured to have undergone a series of surgery on his knee.

There were claims that Tinubu allegedly undergone another knee surgery at the John Hopkins University Hospital in Maryland, the United States.

It was gathered that the former Lagos State Governor returned to the United Kingdom on crutches after undergoing the successful surgery.

Recall that President Muhammadu Buhari had last week paid a ‘goodwill’ visit the APC leader.

Buhari and Tinubu

Just like Buhari, Governor Babajide Sanwo-Olu if Lagos and another member of the House of Representatives, Hon. James Faleke had also visited the former Lagos State governor.
