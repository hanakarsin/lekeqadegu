Sadie Robertson - Age, Family, Bio

Reality star best known as the daughter of Duck Dynasty star Willie Robertson. She appears with Willie and her grandfather Phil Robertson on series. She also finished in second place in the 19th season of Dancing with the Stars.

Before Fame

She was an athletic child and enjoyed playing basketball and running track.

Trivia

Her grandfather's show, Duck Dynasty, was the highest rated reality show on television in 2013.

Family Life

She has five siblings: John Luke, Will, Rebecca, Bella, and Rowdy. She dated Blake Coward from 2014 until 2016. She also dated Austin North in 2018. In June 2019, she got engaged to Christian Huff after less than a year of dating and they tied the knot in November 2019. In May 2021, they welcomed their first child together, a daughter named Honey. In 2023, she and Christian welcomed a second daughter named Haven. 

Associated With

She is the oldest daughter of Korie Robertson.
