Two Cattle Rustlers Arrested In Bauchi

Two cattle rustlers were arrested by the Bauchi State Police Command and 5 cattles were recovered in Durum market, Bauchi Local Government Area.

Police PR Officer, DSP Haruna Mohammed, said when he adressed newsmen: “The command arrested two notorious suspects and recovered five cattle alleged to have been stolen from Jahun in Jigawa State”.

According to Mohammed, the police command also recovered a stolen Honda Accord, model of 2010.

All the members of the local public who have either lost cattle or car, are urged to report to the Estate Divisional Police Station to identify the stolen posession and to collect it.
