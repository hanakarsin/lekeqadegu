Who Are the 'Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve' Performers for 2022?

Every year, 'Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve' puts on a top show with epic performances from the year's biggest stars. Details on the 2022 lineup.

While people often have mixed emotions about saying goodbye to a year during their typical New Year's Eve celebrations, things are quite different with regard to the send-off for 2022.

Many people are thrilled to ring in a new year and hope that things will improve from the dumpster fire that was the past two years.

Article continues below advertisement

ABC is set to put on quite the show with their annual Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve lineup.

The Times Square show will be hosted by Ryan Seacrest and Liza Koshy, and Ciara, D-Nice, and Billy Porter will be co-hosting from afar for celebrations in Disneyland, Los Angeles, and New Orleans respectively. The event will surely get people who are watching home in the festive spirit.

Keep reading to find out who is on the confirmed list of performers.

2023 'New Year's Rockin' Eve' Performers — Times Square

The Times Square performers list started out with four major players: Duran Duran, New Edition, j-hope, and Jax.

Unlike the other cities, which were pre-taped, Duran Duran, New Edition, j-hope (of BTS), and Jax will be performing live.

2023 'New Year's Rockin' Eve' Performers — Los Angeles

While New York City's performer list is a bit slim this year, LA will be bringing some serious heat with the following performers:

  • Wiz Khalifa
  • FINNEAS
  • Armani White
  • Betty Who
  • Dove Cameron
  • Nicky Youre

2023 'New Year's Rockin' Eve' Performers — Disneyland

  • Singer Ciara will be hosting the festivities over at Disneyland in Anaheim, CA., and also taking the stage to perform her hits
  • "Better Thangs" and "Jump." The rest of the lineup includes:
    • Fitz and The Tantrums
    • Maddie & Tae
    • Shaggy
    • Ben Platt
    • Aly & AJ
    • Halle Bailey
    • TOMORROW X TOGETHER (TXT)

    • Lauren Spencer Smith

    2023 'New Year's Rockin' Eve' Performers — New Orleans

    Billy Porter is pulling double duty once again for this year's New Year's Rockin' Eve. Not only is he the host of the New Orleans segment of the show, but he is also performing a medley of his hits!

    We can't wait to see what he has planned for New Year's Eve — no matter how high our expectations are, he's always sure to exceed them!

    Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve celebration will air live on Friday, Dec. 31 starting at 8 p.m. EST. Are you ready to ring in the new year with some incredible performances?
