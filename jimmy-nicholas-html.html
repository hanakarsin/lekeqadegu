Jimmy Nicholas - Trivia, Family, Bio

Television and film actor best known for his role as Evan Hawkins in the 2012 NBC action series Chicago Fire. He has also been cast in the shows The Good Wife and Instinct. He has his own sports podcast titled Everything but the Kitchen Sink.

Before Fame

He began his acting career in 2014. He made his official film debut while acting in the movie Not Cool.

Trivia

The NBC series Chicago Fire was nominated for Favorite Network TV Drama at the 2017 People's Choice Awards. He has garnered a strong following on his jimmynicks Instagram account for his behind-the-scenes and fitness content. 

Family Life

He is based in Chicago, Illinois.

Associated With

He acted in the NBC action series Chicago Fire alongside Taylor Kinney and Christian Stolte.
