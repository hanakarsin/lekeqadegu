Kpapasmurf - Age, Family, Bio

Comedic personality on the web most notable for his contributions to Instagram. His content included comedy skits and lip-sync and dance videos among other random posts.

Before Fame

He acted in plays and enjoyed the arts ever since he was a child. He always loved to capture the attention of people in the most unpredictable way. 

Trivia

He came up with the moniker kpapasmurf due to his obsession with the color blue. In 2017 he was on MTV's Ridiculousness for a video. 

Family Life

He is from Atlanta, Georgia. He has a sister and an older brother.

Associated With

His collaborators on Instagram include Killumantii, Trill Sheik and ChyTheGreatest.
