APC Wins First Polling Unit In Ekiti Election

The Governorship Candidate of All Progressives Congress (APC), Biodun Oyebanji, has secured his first victory in the ongoing governorship election in Ekiti State.

Naija News gathered that Oyebanji polled 20 votes ahead of the governorship candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party, Bisi Kolawole who garnered two votes.

The result is from the polling unit 10, ward 10, Afao/Kajola.

Another candidate like the governorship candidate of the Social Democratic Party, Segun Oni did not get a single vote in the polling unit.

There have been allegations of vote-buying in the electoral process on Saturday, as political parties accuse party agents of voting buying.
