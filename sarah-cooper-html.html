Sarah Cooper - Age, Family, Bio

American comedian and blogger most famous for her satirical corporate news and humor blog The Cooper Review. Her first book, 100 Tricks to Appear Smart in Meetings: How to Get By Without Even Trying, was published in 2016. Her own Netflix special, titled Sarah Cooper: Everything's Fine, was announced in the summer of 2020. 

Before Fame

She earned a theatre scholarship to the University of Maryland. She later became a business student at the Georgia Institute of Technology.

Trivia

She is extremely popular on Twitter, posting comedic videos to her sarahcpr account, which has earned over 2.1 million followers. She previously worked at Google and served as the design lead for Google Docs. 

Family Life

Her mother is half German and her father is half Chinese.

Associated With

She and Necole Kane are both bloggers who grew up in Maryland.
