Amber Wolf - Age, Family, Bio

Gamer and video creator known for her amberwolf20 TikTok account. She primarily posts livestream clips of games like Call of Duty. She frequently sets challenges for herself and engages in pranks if she fails those challenges. Examples include 120 kills in 10 games, or calling her love interest on livestream. Her content has helped her amass 1.7 million fans. 

Before Fame

She became active on TikTok in August of 2020. 

Trivia

She also plays Valorant. Her amberwolf Twitch account is where she does longer livestreams of games. 

Family Life

Associated With
