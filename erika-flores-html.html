Erika Flores - Age, Family, Bio

Initially famous for her role as Colleen Cooper on the popular television series Dr. Quinn, Medicine Woman, Flores later appeared in episodes of CSI: Miami; House; and The Love Boat: The Next Wave.

Before Fame

When she was just twelve years old, she appeared in an episode of Star Trek: The Next Generation.

Trivia

Her made-for-television movie credits include roles in Soul of the Game, Buried Secrets, and Bloodlines: Murder in the Family.

Family Life

Her sister and fellow child actress Melissa Flores also appeared on Dr. Quinn, Medicine Woman. The siblings were born in Grass Valley, California.

Associated With

During the third season of Dr. Quinn, Medicine Woman, Flores left the show and was replaced by fellow actress Jessica Bowman.
