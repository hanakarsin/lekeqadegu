Osiel Vzquez - Age, Family, Bio

Mexican TikTok star and rapper known as Tornillo. He is famous for tracks including "Morena," "Iluminatik" and "Por Unos Centavos" with Doom Deca. He has earned more than 3.9 million followers on his tornillooficial444 account. 

Before Fame

From a very young age, he developed his taste for music. He was influenced by Mexican rap and cumbia.

Trivia

He is part of a collective with 473 Music and Santa Fe Klan. He is also active on his tornillovazquez_ Instagram account.  He posted a TikTok video wearing an Orlando Magic jersey in October of 2022.

Family Life

His real name is Tornillo Vázquez.

Associated With

He recorded a handful of TikTok videos rapping to his own music while wearing a Dennis Rodman, throwback Chicago Bulls jersey.
