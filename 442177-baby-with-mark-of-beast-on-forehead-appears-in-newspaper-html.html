Baby With Mark Of Beast On Forehead Appears In Newspaper

A controversial photo of a 'devil child' with 666 in its curls featured by Swedish paper Svenska Dagbladet caused turmoil among its readers.

Several attentive readers of Sweden’s daily newspaper were shocked after they noticed an unusual photo of a girl, called Lucy, whose name and appearance was thought to be a reference to the devil. Besides, the unusual photo was followed by a questionable email address "rehtom.snatas" – or "Mother of Satan" if read backwards.
