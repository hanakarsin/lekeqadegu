PHOTOS: Bill Gates, Dangote In Kaduna State

One of the world's richest men, Bill Gates, and the president of Dangote Group, Aliko Dangote, are currently in Kaduna state to sign a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) on routine immunization with the governors of six states.

Bill Gates was received by the state governor, Nasir el-Rufai and the Sokoto state governor, Aminu Tambuwal.

The governors of Borno, Bauchi, Yobe and Kano are also in Kaduna, along with the Sultan of Sokoto and the Emir of Kano.
