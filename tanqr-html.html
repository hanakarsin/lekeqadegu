TanqR - Age, Family, Bio

Roblox gaming personality who has earned more than 2.9 million subscribers for YouTube where he posts gameplay footage. His "how many LEVELS can I get in 1 Hour.. (Roblox Arsenal)" video has received over 9.8 million views. 

Before Fame

He was known as TanqRPlays when he posted a debut YouTube video in July of 2015 called "Roblox - Apocalypse Rising Montage - Untitled - TanqR."

Trivia

His first video to surpass half-a-million views was "*NEW* BOSS (Queen Ant) *Drops Rare Loot* & SECRET PORTAL! | Roblox: Booga Booga." Some of his popular videos involved meeting "toxic players" on the Roblox platform.

Family Life

He is from England. He started dating his girlfriend Aimayee in 2018.

Associated With

He once posted a video called "Celestia Vega Plays Roblox - Roblox High School (Real) (Proof)."
