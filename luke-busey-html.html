Luke Busey - Age, Family, Bio

American actor known for his appearances on the Disney comedy series Bunk'd and the reality show Impractical Jokers. He has appeared in over 5 episodes of Bunk'd as the character Jake. He played the character Brian in the horror film Cobweb. 

Before Fame

His birth was announced on a 2010 episode of Entertainment Tonight.

Trivia

He appeared on a 2014 episode of Oprah: Where Are They Now? He has gained a significant following on his lukebusey Instagram page as a result of his acting work. 

Family Life

He is the son of actor Gary Busey. His brother, Jake Busey, is also an actor.

Associated With

He starred alongside Lotus Blossom in the 2012 short film Change of Heart.
