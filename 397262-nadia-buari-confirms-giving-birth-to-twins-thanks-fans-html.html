Nadia Buari Confirms Giving Birth To Twins, Thanks Fans

The report that fair-skinned Ghanaian actress, Nadia Buari had given birth to a set of twin girls in a hospital outside the country surfaced on March 2, 2015.

Though the news was confirmed by her father, Alhaji Sidiku Buari, many were not so convinced as some were of the opinion it was another publicity stunt.

Nadia has finally confirmed the news.

The actress took to Instagram to thank her fans for the love shown to her and her ‘little angels’.

READ ALSO: Jim Iyke’s Boo Nadia Buari Gives Birth To Twins

The note she posted on Instagram reads: “Dear Buttercakes..How wonderful it is to feel cherished and cheered by one’s loved ones! My heart throbs and thrive in joy by the much love you have all shown me and my little angels. We are eternally grateful. May my joy of the moment become your moment of joy as we enjoy this moment of Gods blessings together. To God, to you and all who think forward…love you immensely. Kisses. Love, N.S.B.”

While many were wondering who the baby daddy is, others were speculating Nollywood actor Jim Iyke, who has been romantically linked to the actress might be the father of twins.

Report later surfaced that the father of the twins is someone she went to university with and that her relationship with Jim Iyke ended not long after he proposed to her last year in one of the episodes of his reality TV show, Jim Iyke Unscripted.
