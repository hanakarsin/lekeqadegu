Chris Young (Country Singer) - Age, Family, Bio

Recipient of a contract with RCA Records after his winning finish on the television program Nashville Star. He has released #1 hits like "The Man I Want to Be."

Before Fame

He developed a love for singing in his school choir. During his time in college, he toured and performed more than 150 times.

Trivia

He released the single "Drinkin' Me Lonely" after winning Nashville Star.

Family Life

He is the grandson of Richard Yates, who was a performer on the Louisiana Hayride.

Associated With

He did a cover of the song "Rose in Paradise" with Willie Nelson.
