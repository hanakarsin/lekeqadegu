Lucia Gibson - Age, Family, Bio

Hollywood royalty, she gained fame as the daughter of film star Mel Gibson and singer Oksana Grigorieva. 

Before Fame

Her parents had publicly begun dating in 2009, but by early 2010, they had split. 

Trivia

After her parents' separation, she became the subject of a highly publicized custody battle that ran until August of 2011. 

Family Life

She has eight brothers, Milo, Louis, Christian, Lars, Edward, Thomas, Alexander, and William, and one sister, Hannah. 

Associated With

Some of her famous family members include grandfather Hutton Gibson and great-grandmother Eva Mylott, who was a renowned opera singer. Her brother, Alexander, is the son of Timothy Dalton.
