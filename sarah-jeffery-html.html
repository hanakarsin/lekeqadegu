Sarah Jeffery - Age, Family, Bio

Canadian actress best known as Evie Travis in the show Rogue who went on to join the cast of FOX's Wayward Pines in 2015. In 2018, she began starring in the reboot of the CW's Charmed. She also played Audrey in the Disney's Descendants and Jennifer Lopez's on-screen daughter on the NBC series Shades of Blue. 

Before Fame

She was three years old when she began singing, dancing and acting in musicals and theater. 

Trivia

She performs with a semi-professional dance crew called BODY. 

Family Life

She was born in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. She has a sister named Hannah. She previously dated Nick Hargrove. 

Associated With

She worked with Matthew Espinosa on the film Be Somebody.
