Manyatta MP John Muchiri apologises for using his photos to brand school bus bought through CDF

Manyatta MP John Muchiri has been forced to tender apology following online bashing after he bought school buses using constituency development fund and emblazoned with his photos.

The lawmaker had been trolled by a section of Kenyans for the better part of Wednesday, July 31, as many put him on chopping board over what they termed as serious abuse of office.

According to the Muchiri, the branding and pasting his images and name was specifically for the purposes of handing the 52-seater bus to students of St. Benedict Day Secondary School Kithimu.

Despite the social media evidently going bonkers over the same, the legislator remained completely resolute that his development agenda was on well on course.

"I'm sorry to those who may feel offended but I have five more buses to issue out to our Day Secondary Schools and the projects identification graphics will apply to all then will be removed after official handing over. Thank you all for the great online response towards my projects as this motivates me more," he posted.
