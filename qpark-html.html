Aug 13, 2024 Chauncey Koziol

QPark - Age, Family, Bio

Korean-American Instagram and Vine superstar who became known for over-exaggerating real life situations in his videos. He has over 4.2 million followers on his popular Instagram account. He has also comedically impersonated artists like Lil Pump and 6IX9INE.

Before Fame

He is a graduate of Yale University and previously worked at Morgan Joseph & Co., Inc.

Trivia

He owned a Korean BBQ restaurant. He has earned advertisement deals with companies like Virgin Mobile, Pepsi, Gap, and more. He came out with his first single called "AY AY AY" and it was featured in an article on Billboard in January of 2020. 

Family Life

He's lived in New York City and has a brother. He is in a relationship with Edith Marie, who is featured on his Instagram. They got engaged in July 2021.

Associated With

He collaborated on a Vine ad for VH1 with All American Rejects singer Tyson Ritter.
