Educational background

After graduating from high school, Aria joined Southern Oregon University and pursued a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Theatre. He later went to Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre in New York City and got an acting apprenticeship from the same institution in 2015.

Career history

Shahghasemi worked as a bartender in several New York City restaurants while studying at Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre. He also featured in some theatre productions, including Hamlet.

The actor later became a production assistant in a movie titled Wackademia. However, his big break came when he starred in a 2015 TV series called Unforgettable.
