'Now, It's Time And I'm Here To Spank You Thoroughly'

Nollywood actress cum philanthropist, Tonto Dikeh has threatened controversial investigative journalist, Kemi Olunloyo as she storms Lagos to arrest her.

Kemi Olunloyo and Tonto Dikeh traded words on social a few days ago where the former alleged that the mother of one bought a gun to kill her ex-husband Olakunle Churchill and consumes hard drugs.

Tonto Dikeh urged Kemi Olunloyo to provide proof and name the list of friends who probably gave her the hint that she smokes.

In a post shared on her Instagram page late Saturday night, Tonto Dikeh announced she has landed in Lagos solely to find Kemi Olunloyo.

Tonto Dikeh gave Kemi Olunloyo the option to show up at the address sent to her DM or don’t show up which will make her extremely mad for wasting her fund.

The single mom added that she had never said a thing she cannot do and she’s here in Lagos to spank Kemi Olunloyo thoroughly.

She wrote: “Kemi I have just landed in Lagos solely to find you, You have 20 options and one fact. 1. show up at the address in your DM”

2. “Don’t show and make me waste my funds and time. Now this get me super, super mad1 3. Kemi you need to know I have never said a thing I can’t do, am her to spank you thoroughly”
