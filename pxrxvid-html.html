Paravi Das - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok star, artist, and illustrator who is known for her singing clips as well as her comedic sketches. She shares her creative content through her pxrxvi TikTok account for her over 2.6 million fans. She also works as a TikTok influencer for HBO Max, creating content for their page.   

Before Fame

She first started uploading videos through TikTok in February of 2019 beginning with a comedy sketch about graduating high school. She started going viral in August of 2019 when her acapella cover of "When I Popped Off" earned over two million likes. 

Trivia

She is also known for having worked as a graphic designer for the UCLA Housing Instagram page. She posts modeling photos on her personal paravi Instagram page, where she has accumulated over 160,000 followers. She is a musical theatre major at UCLA with a music industry minor. She always has a yellow profile picture to help her social media pages be associated with positivity and happiness. 

Family Life

She shared a photo with her mother and father in Los Angeles to her Instagram in September of 2019. Paravi Das is her real name.

Associated With

She shared a photo with fellow social media star and fellow UCLA student Demetrius Harmon to her Instagram in September of 2019.
